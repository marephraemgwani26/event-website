About The Fest

A Celebration of Excellence

GWANI 2026 is the premier inter-college cultural and technical fest hosted by Mar Ephraem College of Engineering and Technology. It brings together brilliant minds from various institutions to compete, collaborate, and celebrate.

This year, we are hosting 31 diverse events designed for both Under Graduate (UG) and Post Graduate (PG) students. Join us to make memories, display your creativity, and ignite the spark!

Got Questions?

Who can participate in GWANI '26?

All Bonafide students from recognized Engineering, Arts & Science, and Management colleges can participate.

Is there any registration fee?

Registration fee details will be updated shortly. You can register online to reserve your spot, and payment details will be communicated to your email.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, as long as the event timings do not clash. Please refer to the detailed schedule on the event day.

Will accommodation be provided?

Accommodation requests are handled on a case-by-case basis for outstation participants. Please contact the organizers directly for arrangements.
