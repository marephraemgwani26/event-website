About Gwani '26

Gwani '26 is a National Level Techno Management Fest and a grand celebration of intellect and cultural brilliance, providing a dynamic platform for Undergraduate and Postgraduate students to showcase their unique talents and competitive spirit.

More than 30 Events. 1500+ Participants. ₹1.6 Lakhs in Prizes.

🚌 Transport Facility: College bus facilities are available. Participants can easily use our college transport as a mode to reach the campus.

Event Day Schedule & Guidelines

8:15 AM - 10:00 AM

Registration

Registration begins at 8:15 AM and closes sharply at 10:00 AM. No late registrations will be permitted. A valid College ID is mandatory.

10:00 AM

Inaugural Function

The grand opening ceremony will begin, setting the stage for a day of excitement and competition.

10:30 AM Onwards

Events Commence

All events will start. Participants must report to their venues on time. A participant can take part in only one event (except Spot Games).

Lunch Break

Lunch

Lunch will be provided for all participants. Enjoy a meal and recharge for the afternoon events.

4:00 PM

Valedictory & Prize Distribution

The closing ceremony begins, where winners will be honored and prizes will be distributed.

5:00 PM

Departure

College buses will be available on regular routes and will depart from the campus at 5:00 PM.
